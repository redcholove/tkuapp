About the Minifier

A lot of people ask about how this Minifier was built.

It's essentially a series of regular expressions and replace functions written in PHP, running through Apache on Linux.

If you'd like to get in touch with me - a good way is through Twitter, @willpeavy

Note: to ensure your scripts work after minification - terminate JS statements with a semicolon, and use /* */ syntax for comments.
